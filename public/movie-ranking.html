The Best Movies According to Duncan

My top movies of all time!

The Usual Suspects

Suspenseful, lots of action, and somehow still lighthearted. This is my mom's favorite movie... I am unable to have favorites it seems.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy (Extended Edition)

Just epic all around. Of course, it all stems from the books which are brilliant and transformative.

Fight Club

Anarchist classic. The ultimate glorification of mental health issues. Just a badass movie which, again, comes from a badass book.
